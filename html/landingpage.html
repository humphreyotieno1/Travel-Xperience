About the project

TravelXperience is a project that aims to provide a platform for travel enthusiasts to share their experiences and connect with other like-minded individuals. This project was inspired by the passion for travel and exploration. As avid travelers, we wanted to create a platform that helps others discover unique destinations and plan their own adventures. The idea originated from our own experiences navigating through different cultures, landscapes, and activities. This project serves as our Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing our proficiency in web development and design. It is a testament to our dedication to continuous learning and improvement in the field of software engineering.
